About This Company

Everything You Need. One Trusted Supplier.

Tuyia Hardware & General Supplies is a full-range hardware and merchandise supplier serving Kenya's construction, property development, and business sectors. From foundations to finishing, we stock and supply the materials that keep projects moving and businesses running.

Based at Industrial Area Road A, Nairobi, we serve contractors, developers, government institutions, NGOs, and individual clients — offering competitive pricing, reliable stock availability, and prompt delivery. For large orders, we provide itemised quotations and custom delivery arrangements to your project site.

What We Supply
Our Full Product Range
Building Materials

Cement, steel bars, iron sheets, timber, sand, ballast, and all core construction materials — supplied in bulk or retail quantities to contractors and developers.

Electrical Supplies

Wiring cables, switches, sockets, MCBs, conduit, distribution boards, meters, and complete electrical accessories for residential and commercial installations.

Plumbing Materials

PVC and HDPE pipes, fittings, gate valves, water tanks, sanitary ware, taps, and all plumbing accessories for installation and maintenance works.

Tools & Equipment

Hand tools, power tools, safety gear, PPE, measuring equipment, and site accessories for construction professionals, tradespeople, and maintenance teams.

General Merchandise

Stationery, cleaning products, packaging materials, safety signage, and everyday consumables for offices, schools, and commercial operations.

Bulk & Institutional Supply

Large-volume orders for government ministries, NGOs, developers, and corporates — with itemised quotations, dedicated account management, and site delivery.
