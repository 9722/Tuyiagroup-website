About This Company

Strategic Procurement That Saves You Money

Tuyia Procurement Consultancy helps organisations in Kenya procure smarter — reducing costs, improving supplier quality, and ensuring full legal and regulatory compliance throughout the procurement cycle.

We work with government bodies, NGOs, hospitals, schools, and private corporates, bringing structured methodology and deep market knowledge to every engagement. Whether you need tender support, supplier vetting, or a full procurement policy overhaul, our team delivers results.

Our Services
What We Do For You
Tender Preparation & Submission

We manage the full tendering cycle — identifying suitable tenders, preparing compliant and competitive bids, and submitting on time to government and private sector clients.

Supplier Sourcing & Vetting

We identify, evaluate, and qualify suppliers from Kenya's market to ensure you work only with reliable, value-for-money partners who meet your quality and compliance standards.

Contract Negotiation & Management

Our consultants structure and negotiate contracts to protect your interests — ensuring fair pricing, clear delivery terms, and enforceable warranties from every supplier.

Spend Analysis & Cost Reduction

We analyse your procurement data to identify waste, consolidate spending categories, and implement savings initiatives — delivering measurable cost reductions for your organisation.

Procurement Policy Development

We design and implement procurement policies, approval frameworks, and standard operating procedures tailored to your organisation's size, structure, and regulatory context.

Compliance & Risk Management

We ensure full compliance with Kenya's Public Procurement and Asset Disposal Act, donor requirements, and internal policies — protecting you from audit findings and legal exposure.
