About This Company

Fresh Quality Meat. Delivered Reliably.

Tuyia Meat & Food Supply is a dedicated farm distribution company serving Nairobi's food service and institutional sectors. We source from a vetted network of trusted, licensed livestock suppliers and abattoirs — ensuring every product meets strict quality and food safety standards before delivery.

Whether you run a hotel kitchen, a school canteen, or a hospital catering operation, our team provides the consistency, freshness, and reliability your business depends on. We handle all cold-chain logistics so you receive every order at the right temperature, every time.

What We Supply
Our Product Range & Services
Premium Beef Supply

High-quality fresh beef in bulk or retail cuts — sourced from verified, healthy livestock. Available in a range of cuts including steak, stew, mince, and whole carcass.

Poultry Products

High-quality poultry in bulk or retail cuts — sourced from verified, healthy poultry. Available in Types: Chicken Boneless, Chicken Thigh, Chicken Drumstick, Chicken Wings, Chicken Minced, Gizzard, Etc. We also supply the original kuku Kienyeji.

Goat & Lamb Supply

Fresh goat and lamb meat for restaurants, caterers, event organisers, and households. Regular and on-demand schedules available to suit your business rhythm.

Bulk Institutional Supply

Long-term supply agreements for hospitals, schools, hotels, prisons, and government canteens requiring consistent, large-volume deliveries at contracted pricing.

Cold-Chain Delivery

All meat is transported under refrigerated conditions, maintaining the cold chain from source to your premises — ensuring freshness, food safety, and regulatory compliance.

Quality & Hygiene Assurance

Every batch passes rigorous quality inspection before dispatch. We operate to Kenya Bureau of Standards food safety requirements and good hygienic practice throughout our supply chain.

Custom Orders & Special Cuts

We accommodate special portions, packaging specifications, and bespoke quantities for events, catering contracts, and specific kitchen requirements.
