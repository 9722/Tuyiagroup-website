About This Company

Clean Spaces. Healthy Environments. Every Day.

Tuyia Cleaning & Facility Services provides professional, scheduled cleaning solutions to a wide range of institutional and commercial clients across Nairobi. We are more than a cleaning company — we are a facility management partner committed to hygiene, safety, and consistency.

Our trained, uniformed teams operate under strict quality control protocols. From daily office cleaning to deep sanitation and post-construction cleaning, every engagement is backed by a dedicated supervisor and regular quality inspections to ensure your standards are always met.

Our Services
Comprehensive Cleaning & Facility Solutions
Office & Commercial Cleaning

Daily, weekly, or monthly cleaning for corporate offices, retail spaces, warehouses, and commercial buildings. Flexible scheduling designed around your operations.

Institutional Cleaning

Specialist services for government buildings, hospitals, schools, and universities — meeting the strict hygiene and infection control standards required by each sector.

Deep Disinfection & Sanitisation

Certified disinfection using approved products to eliminate bacteria, viruses, and pathogens — protecting staff and visitors in high-traffic or high-risk environments.

Post-Construction Cleaning

Thorough clean-up after building or renovation works — removing dust, debris, paint residue, and construction waste to prepare spaces for immediate handover and occupation.

Facility Management Advisory

We help organisations develop in-house cleaning programmes, SOPs, and quality assurance frameworks to maintain high hygiene standards consistently without third-party dependency.

Scheduled Maintenance Contracts

Custom maintenance plans — daily, weekly, or monthly — tailored to your facility's footprint, usage intensity, and budget. Long-term contract pricing available.
