About Tuyia Group

A Diversified Group Built for Kenya

Tuyia Group is a Nairobi-based holding company bringing together five specialist subsidiaries under one trusted corporate umbrella. Each company operates with its own dedicated team and expertise — yet unified by shared values of professionalism, integrity, and reliability.

Headquartered at Industrial Area Road A, Nairobi, we serve government institutions, corporates, NGOs, hotels, hospitals, contractors, and individuals across Kenya. Our group structure means you get deep specialist service through a single, accountable partner.
